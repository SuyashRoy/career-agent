Machine Learning Engineer (ML Engineer)

Job Summary:
We are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to design, develop, deploy, and maintain scalable machine learning systems that power intelligent products and business solutions. 
The ideal candidate combines strong software engineering skills with expertise in machine learning algorithms, data processing, and model deployment.

Key Responsibilities:
Design and develop machine learning models for prediction, classification, recommendation, and optimization tasks.
Build scalable data pipelines for model training and inference.
Deploy ML models into production environments and monitor performance.
Collaborate with Data Scientists, AI Engineers, and Product Managers to translate business requirements into ML solutions.
Optimize model performance, latency, and scalability.
Implement MLOps best practices, including CI/CD, model versioning, and monitoring.
Conduct experimentation and A/B testing to improve model effectiveness.
Ensure data quality, model reliability, and compliance with security standards.

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, or related field.
3+ years of experience developing machine learning solutions.
Strong programming skills in Python.
Experience with machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn.
Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
Knowledge of software engineering principles, APIs, and microservices architecture.
Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience with MLOps platforms.
Experience deploying LLMs and generative AI applications.
Knowledge of distributed computing frameworks such as Spark.
