Data Scientist

Job Summary:
We are looking for a Data Scientist to analyze large datasets, uncover actionable insights, and develop predictive models that support strategic decision-making. 
The ideal candidate is highly analytical, business-oriented, and skilled in statistical modeling and experimentation.

Key Responsibilities:
Analyze structured and unstructured data to identify trends and opportunities.
Build predictive and statistical models to solve business problems.
Design and evaluate experiments and A/B tests.
Develop dashboards and reports to communicate insights to stakeholders.
Collaborate with business teams to define success metrics and KPIs.
Clean, validate, and transform data from multiple sources.
Present findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical audiences.

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field.
2+ years of experience in data science or analytics.
Strong knowledge of statistics, probability, and machine learning.
Proficiency in Python and SQL.
Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI.
Ability to communicate complex findings clearly.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience in experimentation and causal inference.
Knowledge of cloud-based analytics platforms.
Experience working with large-scale datasets.
