Marketing Manager

Job Summary:
We are seeking a Marketing Manager to develop and execute marketing strategies that drive brand awareness, customer acquisition, and revenue growth.
The ideal candidate has strong strategic thinking, campaign management, and analytical skills.

Key Responsibilities:
Develop and execute integrated marketing campaigns across digital and offline channels.
Manage brand positioning and messaging.
Plan and oversee content, email, social media, and paid advertising initiatives.
Analyze campaign performance and optimize marketing ROI.
Collaborate with Sales, Product, and Creative teams.
Manage agency relationships and external vendors.
Conduct market research and competitive analysis.
Manage marketing budgets and performance reporting.

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related field.
4+ years of marketing experience.
Proven experience managing multi-channel campaigns.
Strong analytical and project management skills.
Experience with marketing automation and CRM platforms.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience in B2B SaaS or technology marketing.
Knowledge of SEO, SEM, and growth marketing strategies.
Experience leading cross-functional projects.
