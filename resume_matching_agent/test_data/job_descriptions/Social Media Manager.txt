Social Media Manager / Content Creator

Job Summary:
We are seeking a creative and results-driven Social Media Manager / Content Creator to develop engaging content, grow online communities, and strengthen brand presence across social media platforms. 
The ideal candidate combines storytelling, content production, and performance marketing expertise.

Key Responsibilities:
Develop and execute social media content strategies.
Create engaging content including videos, graphics, posts, blogs, and short-form content.
Manage and grow social media channels and communities.
Plan content calendars and campaign schedules.
Monitor social media trends and emerging platform features.
Analyze content performance and optimize engagement.
Collaborate with marketing, design, and product teams.
Manage influencer and creator partnerships when applicable.

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2+ years of experience managing social media accounts and creating digital content.
Strong writing, storytelling, and content creation skills.
Experience with major social media platforms.
Basic understanding of analytics and performance metrics.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience with video editing tools and graphic design software.
Knowledge of SEO and content marketing.
Experience managing paid social campaigns.
