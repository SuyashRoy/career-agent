AI Engineer

Job Summary:
We are seeking an AI Engineer to build and deploy AI-powered applications using large language models (LLMs), generative AI technologies, and advanced machine learning systems. 
The role involves integrating AI capabilities into products and business workflows while ensuring scalability, reliability, and security.

Key Responsibilities:
Design and develop AI-powered applications and intelligent agents.
Build solutions using LLMs, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), vector databases, and AI orchestration frameworks.
Integrate AI services with internal and external systems through APIs.
Fine-tune, evaluate, and optimize AI models.
Implement prompt engineering strategies and AI workflows.
Monitor AI application performance, safety, and cost efficiency.
Collaborate with Product, Engineering, and Data teams to deliver AI solutions.

Required Qualifications:
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning, or related field.
Experience building AI or machine learning applications.
Strong proficiency in Python.
Experience with LLMs and generative AI technologies.
Knowledge of vector databases and semantic search.
Experience deploying applications in cloud environments.

Preferred Qualifications:
Experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar frameworks.
Experience with fine-tuning and model evaluation.
Familiarity with AI governance and responsible AI practices.
